Weigh and Pay

How does it work?

In this vintage store the prices are based on kilos. After you're done with looking around, you put your pile of clothes on one of the scales in the store.

Every piece of clothing has a different color badge attached to it. Each color has a different price per kilo. Once you've weighed your clothes, press one of the color buttons and pay at the checkout!

Sustainability

Vintage clothing is known for its durability and especially in a time where global warming is a serious problem, being as sustainable as possible is a big step towards a healthier earth. By offering vintage clothing The Kilo Store reduces the impact on the environment by not producing any new clothes. Customers are encourages to be aware of their purchases and they are informed about the pros of buying vintage clothing.
